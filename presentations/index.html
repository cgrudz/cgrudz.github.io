I have been searching for a solid alternative to beamer presentations in LaTeX, and to Libre Office, for my mathematical presentations. The main challenge is finding a presentation platform that can handle heavy usage of LaTeX and bibtex, while having a better presentation feel than standard beamer. This has led my to try Reveal.js as an option. This is an ongoing experiment, where I will try hosting a presentation template on my website to test its viability as my primary scientific presentation platform. Along with vanila Reveal.js, I will try converting Jupyter Notebooks into Reveal.js as a possiblitity.
