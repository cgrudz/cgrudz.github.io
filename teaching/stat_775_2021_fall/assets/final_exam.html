Summary

For the final project, you are to to write a term paper in mathematical typesetting software. You can use any software you prefer, but the report is required to be produced in a clean, easy-to-read document with the required document structure below. Figures should be captioned, with clear and easy-to-interpret labels and graphics. The final report must be uploaded in a PDF format to Webcampus. The report should be written in 12 point font, have 1.0 or 1.5 line-spacing and be approximately 5 - 10 pages long, not including references or the appendix of code in the page count.

You are expected to use resources from outside of this course to investigate a topic of your choice and to summarize this topic according to the assignment objectives and rubric. The goal of the assignment will be to read at least one paper of your choice in order to discuss the role of data assimilation in the application, or to discuss the novelty of a technique demonstrated in the work and how it provides novel tools for estimation problems. Suggested topics are:
  • A paper related to your own research – if you are working on an application, you may discuss a paper in which data assimilation techniques have been used in your own research context and what advantages and disadvantages this approach may have.
  • Data assimilation and machine learning – discuss how the Bayesian formalism for data assimilation extends to general machine learning models and hybrid models including both data assimilation and machine learning techniques.
  • Adaptive inflation or localization techniques – discuss different techniques that can be utilized in order to “automatically tune” hyper-parameters in the estimation scheme, from the perspective of, e.g., Bayesian heirarchical models or moment matching techinques.
  • Adaptive (quasi-)Newton algorithms – discuss how, e.g., linesearch techniques or the Levenberg-Marquardt algorithm / trust region approaches differ from the standard Netwon-based optimization.
  • Expectation maximization – discuss how EM has been used to create, e.g., Gaussian mixture models estimating model and observation error statistics in the classical Kalman filter and / or in ensemble Kalman estimators.
  • Multiple data assimilation – discuss the technique of multiple data assimilation and how this relates to annealing in statistical physics and / or a weak-constraint cost function.
  • Reduced precision numerics – discuss the consequences of reducing the precision of numerics on the data assimilation and uncertainty quantification problem. What are the trade-offs and the rationale for reducing precision?
  • Stochastic model reduction – discuss how stochastic reduction of multi-scale physics affects the data assimilation and uncertainty quantification problem.

Your report needs to identify an example where this technique is used in a research or replication paper. Examples from blogs are acceptable for works referenced and may help with discussing code implementations, but you will need to draw your particular example from a scholarly source.
