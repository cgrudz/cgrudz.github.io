Educational Products

Mathematical Theory of Data Assimilation with Applications

Interactive series of lectures given in the London Mathematical Society 2019 Summer Research School on the Mathematics of Climate. This introduces the fundamentals of data assimilation and estimation theory from a Bayesian perspective to new users, with an emphasis on intuition behind the methods. These lectures were developed in collaboration with Alberto Carrassi, Patrick Raanes and Marc Bocquet.

DAPPER

Data Assimilation Package in Python for Experimental Research (DAPPER) is a set of templates for benchmarking the performance of data assimilation (DA) methods. The software provides experimental support and tutorials on data assimilation. I am a contributor to the tutorials on DA in DAPPER.
