I am the Faculty Adviser for the SIAM Student Chapter at the University of Nevada Reno.

I am passionate about global math and science literacy, and promoting meaningful discussions on climate, energy and sustainability. Please see the menus below for information on my activities in the scientific community.

Outreach and service

  • I will be a lecturer in the CIMPA 2021 Mathematics of Climate Science summer school at the African Institute of Mathematical Science (AIMS) in Kigali, Rwanda.
  • I was a co-lecturer with my friend and collaborator Alberto Carrassi in the 2019 LMS-CMI Research School on the Mathematics of Planet Earth. You can find my interactive presentation in my Github repository.

I am a member of the Society of Industrial and Applied Mathematicians and the European Geosciences Union.

I actively perform peer review. See my Publons profile.

I was formerly a cyber-infrastructure volunteer for the Math and Climate Research Network.

The MCRN Hub infrastructure is free for educators and researchers in mathematics and climate to use for online collaboration, hosting, and presenting results. For an introduction to the MCRN Hub, see my video tutorials.
